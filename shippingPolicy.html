Shipping Policy

Since NaukriDwaar is an online placement portal, no physical shipping of products is involved. However, the platform provides services for job seekers and companies to interact digitally. Below is the policy for services provided:

1. Online Services

We offer services through our online platform where job seekers can apply for jobs and companies can post job listings.

2. Account Access

All account-related activities (such as posting jobs or applying for jobs) are carried out online. There are no delivery times associated with these services.

3. Service Availability

Our platform is available 24/7, and you can access our services anytime. Any service interruptions will be communicated to users in advance.
